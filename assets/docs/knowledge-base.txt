## Work Process

### Step 1: Discovery & Problem Mapping
A deep conversation to understand the client's challenge, audience, and goals. No jargon, no assumptions — just clarity.

### Step 2: Solution Design
A curated set of solutions tailored to the client's needs — from lightweight to comprehensive. Options are discussed together to find the best fit for vision, timeline, and budget.

### Step 3: Team Assembly
The ideal mix of freelance developers, 3D artists, and designers is assembled — senior specialists who have delivered together repeatedly. Team size scales from solo to 5–6 experts.

### Step 4: MVP Delivery (1–3 Months)
A clear MVP, timeline, and budget are set. The POC/MVP phase tests the solution in the real world, gathers feedback, and iterates until it perfectly matches client needs and market demands.

### Step 5: Full Development & Iteration
After MVP validation, the project scales — adding features, polishing the experience, and delivering the final product. The goal is not just to meet expectations, but to exceed them.
